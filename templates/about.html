Overview

This website visualises Chelsea FC season data from their foundation in 1905 to the 2016 season. The data is pulled from List of Chelsea F.C. seasons.

Use

The line-chart shows vital season statistics from Chelsea's foundation in 1905 to 2016. The pie-chart shows the majority of time Chelsea have spent in both the 2nd and 1st flight The 1st flight includes the premier league founded in 1992. The table below shows the data in-line season by season. Hover over the legend on the line-graph to get a better visual indication. Click on segments of the pie-chart to isolate the data in the line-chart and table.

Technology

  • Mongodb. A 'no-sql' database, used to store the data.
  • Flask for the backend. Used to pass the data from Mongodb to the JS and to serve the webpage
  • Crossfilter. To mould the data to our need and to make use of it's two-way-binding.
  • D3.js & DC.js. To render our charts.
  • Bootstrap. To act as a skeleton for our HTML elements.
  • JQuery. To more easily manipulate the dom. Particularly helped with the loading page.
